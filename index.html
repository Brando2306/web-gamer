THE VISION

Giblatön was now a very different place, the original planet had been transformed. Immense forests covered the land, and its huge trees sheltered an abundant fauna, the water flowing from the mountains formed meandering rivers of crystalline waters and the sound of life flowed with harmony from anywhere on the planet. The great mountain of Lamaintula, majestic as ever, still dominated the place, and at its highest point, the enormous silhouettes of the ten gods seemed to outline a peculiar rocky massif. Giblon had created his nine gods with a mission, with a task, each one had to execute it with effort and rigour, and seeing what that planet had become, they had fulfilled their objective.
However, perhaps all that work could be threatened and the concern of Giblon, the god of gods, was evident. His powerful Galactic Orb had transmitted a prophetic vision, a threat. Giblatön was in danger, something terrible was going to happen. He could see a kind of... war? He saw many golems, but he also observed other kinds of seemingly ferocious creatures.
INNATE VIRTUES

In his quest to protect Giblatön, the Creator extracted from his Galactic Orb 3 Innate Virtues, each created for the sole purpose of covering an essential aspect in battle. Each of these 3 Innate Virtues provided its possessor with supernatural powers and enhanced his abilities. Annihilating Power, Unbreakable Defense and Supreme Intelligence were the virtues given to the gods of war: Golowar, Tarblok and Tsatsako, respectively. The virtues were integrated directly into the genes of these three gods. All their power circulated within them, it was part of their nature. Between them, they had perfect synergy. War was their ultimate task. And the most important thing... These Innate Virtues were inheritable.
THE 10 GOLEM GODS

GIBLON, THE GOLEM CREATOR:
Giblon, the Golem Creator was the first ever golem to exist, the creator of everything. He was a huge majestic beast with unparalleled power and knowledge. The Galactic Orb he possessed, had control over time and life. He knew the past and could observe the future through it. After millions of years alone in Mount Lamaintula, he had a terrifying sighting through his orb. Something terrible was going to happen in the near future. He knew he had to do something to preserve the state of things This is why he finally decided to use the power of the Orb to create the other Gods to help him rule the planet and its future. Each of them was created with his own purpose and power, but all were extremely powerful.
DEMANTUR, THE DIAMOND GOD:
Demantur, The Diamond God, it's one of the Gods created inside Mount Lamaintula using the power of the Galactic Orb. His body consisted entirely of pure diamond. His sword, 'Gobl's Edge', turned everything it touched into diamond, penetrating it and exploding it into a thousand crystals. He was a sturdy, heavy, unbreakable golem. Demantur created great diamond dungeons, where the golems would live, protected by abysmal walls and towers that sealed the golem territory. No golem dared to look him in the eyes, it was said that your body would become a solid block of diamond forever.
LEVIATON, THE GRAVITY GOLEM:
Its entire body was composed of the gravitational rocks that surrounded the planet of Giblatön. The gravitational field that this God possesses allowes him to rebuild his body under any circumstance. He could increase in size by grabbing rocks and could push them out of his body if he needed to shrink. Nobody dares to be near him, you could feel his huge gravity power from large distances. If you get close to Leviaton, you would probably end up separated into 1 million pieces.
KHAOS, THE FIRE GOD:
Khaos was created from Eternal Ember rocks that were submerged under the lava of Mount Lamaintula. His entire body was engulfed in a flame that would never ever extinct, and he burned at temperatures no other golem could withstand. He was in charge of guarding the access to the Mount, where the gods gathered. If you aroused his wrath, with his huge Golemite steel ax, he would open huge breaches in the ground.
RUDE GOLEMS: THE STORY

In the twilight of the Genesis era, at the dawn of the end of creation, in a dark and unknown time, Giblatön roared with the shattering power of its inhabitants. Forged in the bowels of the earth with the power of the almighty creator and gestated in the boiling womb of the interior of Mount Lamaintula, those creatures disturbed the silence of the place. Minerals, rock and strange materials only known there gave shape to enormous beings with the innate virtue of creating chaos and destruction wherever they dwelled, golems were called. Rude Golems.
GIBLATÖN: ORIGIN

In the confines of the Solana pentagalaxy, in its darkest and most unexplored vortex, after the collision of two great stars, a small and inert planet emerged. Eight suns and eight moons provided light and darkness to Giblatön, a territory all inhospitable and disturbing. Its atmosphere was dense and seemed to vibrate to the sound of the multitude of volcanoes that with their roar tinged the environment with colourful gaseous emanations and incandescent materials. It was a strange place, different, devoid of life, and yet magical, despite its apparent lack of gravity in which the rocks seemed to want to float, an inner force, for millions of years, wanted to catch something...
GIBLON: GENESIS

Eight suns, eight moons, eight eclipses... An unique and disturbing fact, a call. At the highest point of Mount Lamaintula, eight runes that remained hidden in large granite rocks began to shine brightly. A silent chant of intense colour began to dye that mountain with light, it was a chant of attraction, hypnotic and powerful attraction. The light of the mountain contrasted with the perpetual darkness of the eclipsed night, until suddenly a small light appeared in space, an interstellar fragment wrapped in a bright and majestic aura that travelled swiftly towards Giblatön. And at last, with a roar that echoed throughout the planet, the two lights merged. It would be difficult to say whether the being that now crowned Mount Lamaintula was born from the bowels of the earth or came from the ends of the universe,
even he did not know, but there it was. The runes now illuminated the place with more strength, it was the magical announcement of a birth. Giblon had been born and a new era was beginning before his feet… GODS: THE BEGINNING

Giblon, the Golem Creator, had been wandering around his inert planet for many years (thousands, perhaps millions), his solitary existence was not an easy task, his Galactic Orb was his guide and his oracle, and his mission was to make Giblatön a living and powerful planet within Solana.

For a long time, he had tried to sow the germ of life, to bring order to the space and physics of his planet, to its elements, its matter, but despite his extreme power, it was a Dantesque task, something he now knew he couldn’t do alone.

Being immune to all forms of physical harm makes him almost invincible. He is also very Rude.

ROOTON, THE NATURE GOD:
Rooton is the god in charge of creating and protecting the flora and fauna of Giblatön. It's formed by branches of millenary trees. His body acts as a home for various creatures present on the Golems' planet. With the Life Cane, he is able to change the environment, return to live dead flora and when he's mad, even drain others' spirits. He is the biggest in size of all the Gods, he does not like to talk much and he will cage you in a wall of thick branches and spikes forever if you dare to disturb him.
G7-XSOL, THE OTHERWORLDLY GOD:
A ball of fire impacted at a high speed against Giblatön many, many years ago. It was a huge spaceship from another universe. This great ship was built with galactic materials that seemed to have special qualities and incredible hardness. Only the Creator God was able to observe this event, and in his quest to create other gods, he decided to use the remains of this great ship to give life to the seventh God. G-7 XSOL was a mechanized golem, different from all others. It has thrusters all over its body that allows him to move through the air at great speeds. His hydraulic systems fed with radioactive material allowed him to carry heavy weights and fly without any difficulty, it is also very hard to damage, his armor is extremely resistant. A mecha golem from another universe. Curious, isn't it?
GOLOWAR, THE ATTACK GOD:
Inside this God there is only war. He was born to battle. The huge sword he handles not only serves to slice through his enemies, but it gives him a relentless war instict. Golowar has a supernatural speed that allows him to move fast and forcefully, he is way more agile than anyone else. The strength that his sword gives him makes him a killing machine. He is a melee master He loves to annihilate, to destroy, anger is part of him. No matter what stands in front of him, he could instantly end its life. He is the holder of the Annihilating Power.
